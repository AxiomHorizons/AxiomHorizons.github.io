How Axiom Horizons works

Learn how we can help you make more and what the process is.
Step One

Find a Corp-to-Corp opportunity

Find to C2C jobs from any job site, such as Indeed.com. It can be any job that meets the following criteria:

  • Must be a full-time Corp-to-Corp (C2C) contract position.
  • The role must be within Information Technology. For example a developer, project manager, tester, business analyst, engineer, or network administrator.
  • The rate must be at least $50 per hour.
  • Must be based in the United States. (Sorry, no H-1B visas at this time)

Step Two

Submit the job(s) to Axiom Horizons

Send us the jobs you wish to apply to. We will schedule interviews for you and perform a company background check on any unknown company. When you attend the interview, remember that C2C jobs typically pay much more than other hourly jobs when negotiating your rate.

Step Three

Finish On-Boarding

After you are offered a position, choose which packages you would like for medical, dental, retirement savings, and more. Once, selected, we'll show you what your salary will be.

Because you were a part of the process in finding the job, our overhead is lower and, therefore, can offer you a higher salary. But, since you'll be a full-time employee with us, you won't lose benefits that you're accustomed to having.
