What is Axiom Horizons?

We hire contractors as salaried employees

Think of us like a service that contractors can use to save money on group health insurance, invoicing, accounting, business insurance and more.

Are you a recruiter or staffing agency?

No, we do not actively recruit

Workers are responsible for finding their own opportunities. This gives them the freedom to choose which projects they work.

Do you negotiate on behalf of the worker?

Workers do the negotiating

The workers find the jobs, set up interviews, and negotiate their pay. Once the pay is decided, we calculate the worker's salary. If you're curious what that looks like, you can check out our Salary Calculator.

Does the worker represent Axiom Horizons?

We represent ourselves

While the worker is technically our employee so we can offer them group health insurance, they are not an authorized representative of Axiom Horizons. Therefore, while they can sign non-disclosure and non-compete contracts, an authorized representative of Axiom Horizons will have to review and sign any statement-of-work contract.
